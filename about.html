About Me

I am the guy who tried a lot of different stuff in life until I found the one which brings me the most pleasure while doing it. And, that is UX/UI design and making those designs go live on the web, respectively, frontend web development. I am fluent in English with the highest level of proficiency and I find my communication and management skills very high as I have also studied Management. I am not limited when it comes to complexity of the product. So, I am capable of creating anything from the simple website or app to the most complex softwares. My proficiency is related to using several softwares in my workflow and I am always keen on learning new technologies to widen the range of my capabilities.

So far my experience is related to about 2 years of working as a freelancer and about half a year as a full-time employee in the software development company.

I love helping people to solve their problems with my creativity. I love designing the products that are going to easen the things for community. As I am UI/UX designer with the frontend web development knowledge, I am happy when I am able to even develop the products I designed myself with all my vision, animations and extras I imagined. Being connected with both fields gives you an opportunity to better understand the process and to make the best out of it.
